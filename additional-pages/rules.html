Rules

  1. Start your game
  2. Select which continent you'd like to be quizzed on.
  3. You will be asked 10 questions per round & you must select one answer for each question.
  4. If you get it right you will get an alert to tell you!
  5. If you get it wrong you will be told the answer so that you can learn for next time.
  6. Once you've answered all 10 questions you will be given your total score.
  7. Save this score to our high scores table to see how you improve!
