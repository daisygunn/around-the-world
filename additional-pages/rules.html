Rules

  1. Start your game
  2. You will be asked 10 questions per round & you must select one answer for each question.
  3. If you get it right you will hear a sound alert & the answer will turn green.
  4. If you get it wrong you will hear a different sound & the answer will turn red.
  5. You will see your total score in the top right corner.
  6. Once you have answered all 10 questions you will be able to save this score to our high scores table to see how you improve!
